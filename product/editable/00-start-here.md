# The Freelance Client Kit

## Start Here

Welcome — and thank you for buying the Freelance Client Kit.

This kit exists to solve one problem: **most freelancers lose money not because their work is bad, but because their client process is improvised.** Vague proposals, missing kickoff steps, awkward money conversations, scope creep with no script to push back — each one quietly costs you hours and dollars.

Everything in this kit is a plug-and-play system. Copy it, adapt the wording to your voice, and use it on your very next client.

## How to Get Results in the Next 7 Days

1. **Today:** Fill out the Rate-Setting Worksheet (#8). Most freelancers discover they're undercharging by 20–40%.
2. **Before your next prospect call:** Read the Discovery Call Script (#2) once. You don't need to memorize it — just follow the five phases.
3. **On your next quote:** Use the Proposal Template (#3) verbatim. It's structured to sell the outcome, not the hours.
4. **When they say yes:** Run the Onboarding Checklist (#1) top to bottom. This is where you look more professional than 90% of your competition.
5. **Every Friday:** Send the Weekly Status Update (#7). It takes 5 minutes and eliminates the "just checking in..." emails from clients.
