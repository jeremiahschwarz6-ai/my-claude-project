# $3K/Month Plan — Walkthrough Video Business

Goal: at least $3,000/month within 30 days, built so most of it repeats next month
without restarting from zero. The one-week $1K sprint (see
`1k-week-photo-to-video-gameplan.md`) is Week 1 of this plan.

---

## 1. The Revenue Math (pick a path, know your numbers)

Product ladder:

| Product | Price | Your time | Notes |
|---------|-------|-----------|-------|
| Photo-to-video tour (single) | $99 launch → $149 | ~1 hr | Entry point, sells fast |
| Three-pack | $249 → $399 | ~3 hrs | Better per-sale revenue |
| **Monthly retainer** | **$249–299/mo** | 2–4 videos/mo | **The engine — every buyer gets pitched this** |
| Filmed walkthrough (your original service) | $299–399 | ~4 hrs | Upsell for premium listings |
| Brokerage bulk deal | $79/listing, 10+ min | batch | One deal = $790+ |

Three realistic paths to $3K:

- **Path A — retainer-heavy (best):** 12 agents at $249/mo = $2,988. Almost all
  of it repeats automatically next month.
- **Path B — mixed (most likely):** 6 retainers ($1,494) + 10 singles/packs
  (~$1,200) + 1 filmed walkthrough ($299) ≈ $3,000. About half repeats.
- **Path C — brokerage-led:** 2 brokerage bulk deals ($1,580) + 5 retainers
  ($1,245) + a couple singles ≈ $3,000.

**The rule that makes this durable: every one-off buyer gets the retainer pitch
at delivery.** "You liked this — $249/month, every new listing you get becomes a
video tour automatically, priority 24h delivery." Convert 1 in 3 buyers and the
recurring base builds itself.

## 2. The Funnel Numbers (work backwards)

- ~4% of personalized touches become buyers → **~30 buyers needs ~750 touches/month
  ≈ 35/day, 5 days a week.** That is the job. Everything else is fulfillment.
- Referrals and reposted client videos start replacing cold volume in weeks 3–4 —
  every delivery must ask for one intro (next video 50% off).

Track these 4 numbers weekly (a scorecard beats motivation):
1. Outreach touches sent
2. Replies / demos sent
3. Sales closed ($ one-off vs $ recurring)
4. **MRR (monthly recurring revenue)** — the only number that has to grow every week

## 3. Week-by-Week

### Week 1 — Cash sprint (already planned)
Run the $1K week exactly as written: demo, payment link, 40 touches/day, deliver
in 24h. Outcome: ~$1K, 8–12 paying clients, 1–3 first retainers.

### Week 2 — Double down on what converted
- Look at Week 1 data: which segment replied most (agents / FSBO / Airbnb hosts /
  brokerages)? Put 70% of outreach volume there.
- Raise single price to $149 for new buyers (launch price "expired" — scarcity was real).
- Pitch the retainer to every Week-1 buyer at their delivery or 3 days after.
- Book one brokerage office presentation ("how top agents use video to win
  listings" + live demo of one of their listings, with permission).
- Target: $700–900 new revenue, MRR ≥ $750 (3 retainers).

### Week 3 — Systemize + social proof
- Template the pipeline: photo selection checklist, animation prompt presets,
  edit template, delivery email template. Video #10 should take 45 minutes.
- Publish 2 case-study posts with real numbers from Week 1–2 clients
  ("[Agent]'s listing: 11k views, 6 showing requests from one $99 video").
- Activate referrals hard: personally ask your 3 happiest clients for one intro each.
- Add the filmed-walkthrough upsell for premium listings ($299+): your original
  craft, now sold to a warm list instead of cold.
- Target: MRR ≥ $1,500 (6 retainers), $600+ one-off.

### Week 4 — Lock the base
- Retainer close-out: offer every active client "lock $249/mo before singles go
  to $149 each" — annual-style framing without the commitment fear.
- Close the brokerage deal from Week 2's presentation.
- Raise capacity: if you're at 25+ videos/month, outsource editing assembly to a
  contract editor ($15–25/video) — you keep selling and doing the animation passes.
- Target: MRR ≥ $2,000 exiting the month, total month ≥ $3,000.

## 4. Costs & Capacity Reality Check

- **Credits:** ~150–400 credits per video → 25–30 videos/month ≈ 6,000–10,000
  credits. At that volume the Ultra plan ($129/mo, 3,000 credits) plus top-ups —
  or Ultra annual ($99/mo) — beats one-time packs. Revisit once you're actually
  fulfilling 15+ videos/month; until then Plus (1,000 credits) + a top-up is fine.
  Keep credit + music-license costs under 15% of revenue.
- **Time:** 35 touches/day ≈ 2 hrs; fulfillment at 1 hr/video ≈ 25–30 hrs/month;
  total ≈ 15 hrs/week. If you have less time than that, cut outreach segments,
  not delivery speed — 24h delivery is the reputation.
- **Music licensing:** get one Artlist/Epidemic subscription (~$15–30/mo) so every
  deliverable is safe for clients to post with ads behind it.

## 5. After $3K — What Makes Month 2 Easier Than Month 1

1. MRR base means you start the month at $1,500–2,500 instead of $0.
2. Every client's posted video is a permanent ad with your name on it.
3. Raise prices again at capacity: booked 2+ weeks out = underpriced.
4. The next ceiling is fulfillment, not sales — solve it with the contract editor
   first, a second animator later. Do not slow down selling to edit.
