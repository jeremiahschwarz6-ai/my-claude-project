# Walkthrough Video Business — Client Acquisition Content Pack

Ready-to-use content for getting real estate walkthrough video clients. Swap in your
name, city, and links wherever you see [brackets]. If your niche isn't real estate,
the structure still works — replace "agents/listings" with your buyer and their asset.

---

## 1. Positioning (use this everywhere)

**One-liner:**
> "I help [city] real estate agents win more listings with cinematic walkthrough
> videos that make sellers say 'I want YOU to sell my home.'"

Key insight to build all content around: **agents don't buy videos — they buy more
listings and faster sales.** Every piece of content should connect video → outcome
(more showings, higher offers, listing presentations that win).

### 2b. Instagram/Facebook DM (shorter, casual)

> Hey [Name]! Love how you presented [address] — that kitchen deserves video 🎥
> I shoot walkthrough videos for [city] agents. Want me to do your next listing
> free so you can test it? No strings — you keep the footage either way.

### 2c. Follow-up (3–4 days later, no reply)

> Hi [Name] — following up in case this got buried. One of the agents I work
> with, [client name], had [result: e.g., "3x the showing requests"] after we
> added video to her listing. Happy to do the same test run for you, free.
> If now's not the time, no worries — when's your next big listing going live?

### 2d. Follow-up (the "give value anyway" touch, 1–2 weeks later)

> Hi [Name] — no pitch this time. I put together a one-pager on the 3 things
> that make listing videos actually generate showings (most videos get all 3
> wrong): [link/attachment]. Use it with whoever shoots your videos. If you ever
> want me behind the camera, you know where to find me.

**Outreach math:** aim for 10–20 personalized touches/day. Expect 5–10% reply
rate on well-personalized messages. This is a numbers game with a compounding
referral tail — your first 5 clients come from outreach, the next 20 come from
them.

## 3. Short-Form Video Scripts (Reels / TikTok / Shorts)

Post these to attract agents. The audience is **agents, not home buyers**.

### Script 1 — Before/After (highest converting format)
- HOOK (0–2s): "This listing sat for 40 days. Then we did this."
- Show phone-quality photos → cut to your cinematic walkthrough
- "Same house. Same price. New story."
- CTA: "If you're an agent in [city], DM me 'VIDEO' and I'll shoot your next listing."

### Script 2 — Educational authority
- HOOK: "3 reasons your listing video isn't getting showings"
- 1: "It starts with the front door. Buyers decide in 3 seconds — open with the money shot."
- 2: "It's 4 minutes long. Nobody finishes it. 60–90 seconds, best rooms only."
- 3: "No people, no story. Movement sells space."
- CTA: "Follow for more, or DM me if you'd rather I just handle it."

### Script 3 — Behind the scenes
- HOOK: "POV: shooting a $[X]M listing at golden hour"
- Fast cuts of your setup, gimbal moves, drone launch, best final shots
- Text overlay: "This is what your sellers see when they pick their agent."
- CTA: "Agents — this could be your listing. Link in bio."

### Script 4 — Client result (social proof)
- HOOK: "My client listed this home on Thursday. It was under contract by Monday."
- Show clips of the walkthrough
- "[Agent name] used this video on [platform] — [N] views, [N] showing requests."
- CTA: "Want the same for your next listing? DM 'BOOK'."

### Script 5 — The listing-presentation angle
- HOOK: "Agents: this wins you the listing before you even talk price."
- "When a seller interviews 3 agents and only one shows up with THIS…" (play best clip)
- "…the seller stops comparing commissions and starts imagining their home in the video."
- CTA: "I'll build you a listing-presentation reel from your first shoot. DM me."

**Posting rhythm:** 3–4 shorts/week. Rotate: 2 portfolio/before-after, 1 educational,
1 social proof or BTS. Every video ends with a DM keyword CTA — keywords make leads
self-identify and are easy to track.

## 5. Lead Magnet (for template 2d and your bio link)

Create a one-page PDF: **"The 3-Second Rule: Why Buyers Skip Your Listing Video
(and the 5-Shot Formula That Fixes It)"**
- Gate it behind an email address on a simple landing page
- Every download is a warm lead → follow up within 24h with template 2a energy

## 6. Referral Engine (your real growth lever)

- After every delivered video, send: "If you know one agent who'd want this,
  intro us and your next shoot is 50% off."
- Give every client a co-branded cut of the video to post — their audience is
  full of other agents.
- Target **brokerages, not just agents**: one office deal = 10–30 agents. Offer
  the broker a lunch-and-learn ("How top agents use video to win listings") and
  shoot one office listing free as the demo.

## 7. Packaging for Scale (stop selling one-off videos)

| Package | What's in it | Why it scales |
|---------|-------------|---------------|
| Basic | 60–90s walkthrough, 48h delivery | Entry point / free-shoot upsell |
| Signature | Walkthrough + drone + 3 vertical cuts for social | Higher ticket, same shoot day |
| Retainer | 4–8 listings/month, priority booking | **Predictable revenue — push everyone here** |

Retainers are how you scale: 5 agents on a monthly retainer beats 20 one-off
clients for the same revenue with a fraction of the sales effort.

## 8. Operational Scaling Checklist (when demand outgrows you)

1. **Systemize first:** shot-list template per property type, preset LUTs/grades,
   editing templates. Cut edit time per video before adding people.
2. **Outsource editing** (your first hire — contract editors, per-video rate).
   You keep shooting and selling; editing is the most delegable piece.
3. **Raise prices** every time you're booked 2+ weeks out. Booked solid means underpriced.
4. **Hire a second shooter** trained on your shot list only after editing is delegated.
5. **Track 3 numbers weekly:** outreach sent, discovery calls booked, revenue per
   shoot hour. Scale whatever moves them.
